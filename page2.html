The Sky Dragon of Osiris


The Sky Dragon of Osiris (also known as Slifer the Sky Dragon) is one of the God cards made by Pegasus J. Crawford, who is the in-story creator of "Duel Monsters".Slifer the Sky Dragon, known as Sky Dragon of Osiris in the Japanese version, is a character version of the card "Slifer the Sky Dragon". Slifer appears in the manga and anime, as one of the most powerful monster spirits, the Egyptian Gods. In the Japanese anime and manga, its card is called "Saint Dragon - The God of Osiris".
