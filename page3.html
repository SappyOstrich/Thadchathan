The Winged Dragon of Ra


The Winged God Dragon of Ra (also known as The Winged Dragon of Ra or The Sun Dragon Ra) is one of the God cards made by Pegasus J. Crawford, who is the in-story creator of "Duel Monsters". It ranks higher than the other two God cards, being invulnerable to their powers. In the Battle City arc, this card is owned by Marik Ishtar. The possession later goes to Yugi Mutou after he won the Battle City Tournament.
