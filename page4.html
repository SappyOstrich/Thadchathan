Obelisk the Tormentor


The Giant God Soldier of Obelisk (also known as Obelisk the Tormentor) is one of the God cards made by Pegasus J. Crawford, who is the in-story creator of "Duel Monsters". Ishizu Ishtar lends the card to Seto Kaiba days before the Battle City Tournament. Kaiba later gains total ownership of the card after defeating Ishizu in a duel. Yugi Mutou then wins over the card after defeating Kaiba in the Battle City Semi-Finals.
